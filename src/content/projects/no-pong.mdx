<ProjectSection>
  <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={8} heading="Natural brand positioning">
    <p>No Pong was positioned as a natural deodorant brand for customers seeking effective, low-tox alternatives with less plastic waste.</p>
  </ProjectText>
  <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={12} heading="Identity direction">
    <p>The visual language was designed to be clean, playful, and approachable, using bold typography and soft tones to signal wellness and everyday usability.</p>
  </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection>
  <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={8} heading="Packaging approach">
    <p>Packaging used a minimal, recyclable tin format with circular labels that mirror the product form and support clear shelf recognition.</p>
  </ProjectText>
  <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={12} heading="Variant system">
    <p>A consistent product system was created across original, bicarb-free, and vegan ranges, each with distinct colour coding within a unified brand architecture.</p>
  </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection>
  <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={8} heading="Result">
    <p>The outcome is a cohesive identity that clearly communicates natural ingredients, sustainability intent, and approachable product performance.</p>
  </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>
