<ProjectSection>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={8} heading="Platform concept and UX scope">
        <p>Erni was built to help individuals monetise spare time by offering services to others, with a streamlined experience for both providers and customers.</p>
    </ProjectText>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={12} heading="Role and delivery">
        <p>I worked as UI/UX designer, crafting key interfaces and user flows to improve listing, booking, and service management across the platform.</p>
    </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>






<ProjectSection>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={5} heading="Service listing and scheduling">
        <p>A calendar-led management flow allowed providers to set availability, rates, and service offerings quickly, with transparent scheduling at the core.</p>
    </ProjectText>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={9} heading="Guided booking flow">
        <p>The customer journey used a guided step-by-step booking flow with clear action cues and review signals to reduce friction.</p>
    </ProjectText>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={13} heading="Discovery and trust signals">
        <p>Filtering by location, pricing, and availability improved matching, while profile detail, ratings, and reviews helped build trust and informed choices.</p>
    </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>


<ProjectSection>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={8} heading="Outcome">
        <p>The resulting interface delivered a cohesive and intuitive experience for booking and availability management, though the startup did not secure funding to progress.</p>
    </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>
