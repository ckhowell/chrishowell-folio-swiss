<ProjectSection>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={5} heading="Service listing and scheduling">
        <p>A calendar-led management flow allowed providers to set availability, rates, and services quickly while keeping scheduling transparent for customers.</p>
    </ProjectText>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={9} heading="Guided booking flow">
        <p>The customer booking journey used clear steps, action cues, and reviews to reduce friction and support confident decisions.</p>
    </ProjectText>
    <ProjectText colSpan={4} colStart={13} heading="Discovery and trust signals">
        <p>Filtering by location, price, and availability improved matching, while profile content and ratings helped users evaluate providers quickly.</p>
    </ProjectText>
</ProjectSection>
